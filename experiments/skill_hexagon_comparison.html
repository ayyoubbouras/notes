Hexagon of market value, not just skill.

This chart compares three profiles on the same six axes: you now, a strong AI-in-production builder, and the highest-value hybrid that combines both. The goal is not to say one person is "better" overall. It’s to show where the market pays a premium: the overlap between structured knowledge depth and production AI capability.

Core takeaway

Your semantic profile is already rare. The comp unlock comes from combining it with evals, orchestration, and production delivery.

In other words: do not abandon the ontology/KG side. Add the missing production-AI half.

What the shapes mean

You now High structure, high conceptual depth, lower production leverage than your upside allows.
AI in production person Strong shipping ability, but often lacks the semantic and modeling depth that creates long-term moat.
Highest-value hybrid The premium profile: enough semantics to structure reality, enough production AI to make it useful at scale.

Interpretation

  • Your strongest edge is not generic AI ability. It is structured intelligence.
  • The production operator’s advantage is speed, deployment, evals, and operational trust.
  • The market pays most when both shapes overlap into one person.
